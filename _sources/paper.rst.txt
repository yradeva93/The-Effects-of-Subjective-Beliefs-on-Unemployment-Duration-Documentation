.. _paper:

*********************************
Research Paper and Project Review
*********************************


The directory *src/paper* contains the following files:

    * :file:`research_paper.tex` contains the actual paper, replicating the main tables, figures and findings of the project The Effect of (Biased) Beliefs on Unemployment Duration.
    * :file:`project_review.tex` contains a detailed explanation of the structure of the project, its strenghts and weaknesses.
    * :file:`refs.bib` contains the references and bibliography used throughout the project by: `research_paper.tex`, `project_review.tex`, and the Documentation.
