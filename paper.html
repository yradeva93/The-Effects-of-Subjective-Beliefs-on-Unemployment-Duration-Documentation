Research Paper and Project Review¶

The directory src/paper contains the following files:

  • research_paper.tex contains the actual paper, replicating the main tables, figures and findings of the project The Effect of (Biased) Beliefs on Unemployment Duration.
  • project_review.tex contains a detailed explanation of the structure of the project, its strenghts and weaknesses.
  • refs.bib contains the references and bibliography used throughout the project by: research_paper.tex, project_review.tex, and the Documentation.
